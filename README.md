# Serein Couture

## Go-to-Market Strategy (Store Owners, Factories, and B2B Partners)

This plan is focused on attracting and converting **store owners, factories, distributors, and other B2B partners** for a project that includes an AI-powered **calling bot**.

## 1) Ideal Customer Profiles (ICP)

### Primary ICP
- **Store owners / retailers**: Need better sourcing speed, dependable inventory, and faster supplier communication.
- **Factories / manufacturers**: Need qualified buyer demand and predictable production pipeline.
- **Wholesalers / distributors**: Need matching between retail demand and factory capacity.

### Secondary ICP
- E-commerce private-label brands.
- Procurement agencies and sourcing consultants.
- Logistics and fulfillment partners.

## 2) Value Proposition by Segment

### Store owners
- Faster supplier onboarding.
- Better pricing visibility.
- Less manual follow-up through automated call workflows.

### Factories
- Higher volume of qualified buyer inquiries.
- Automated quote follow-up and appointment booking.
- Improved demand forecasting signals.

### Distributors and partners
- Unified flow from lead capture to qualification to sales handoff.
- Faster communication cycle and reduced drop-off.
- Higher conversion from inquiry to confirmed order.

## 3) Positioning and Messaging

### Positioning statement
"A B2B growth engine that helps stores and factories connect faster, qualify better, and close more deals with AI-assisted calling workflows."

### Messaging pillars
1. **Speed**: Contact and qualify prospects in hours, not days.
2. **Trust**: Human-reviewed scripts, transparent call logs, and compliance-first controls.
3. **Revenue**: More qualified meetings, quotes, and repeat orders.

## 4) Channel Strategy

### Outbound (primary in first 90 days)
- Build segmented lead lists by geography and category.
- Multi-step sequence: email → LinkedIn → calling bot → human closer.
- Use verticalized scripts (fashion, textile, private label, accessories).

### Inbound
- Publish case studies and quick wins.
- Build keyword pages (example: "AI calling bot for wholesale sourcing").
- Run educational content around compliant AI outreach.

### Partnerships
- Trade associations and sourcing consultants.
- ERP/logistics co-marketing webinars.
- Referral partner incentives.

## 5) Calling Bot Strategy

### Core use cases
- Lead qualification (budget, timeline, volume, decision authority).
- Lead reactivation for inactive opportunities.
- Appointment scheduling and reminder calls.
- Post-order status updates and check-ins.

### Conversation design
- 20-second segment-specific opener.
- Qualification questions and branching paths.
- Objection handling blocks.
- Strong CTA: schedule demo, request quote, or transfer to human.

### Compliance baseline
- Calling-hour controls by local timezone.
- Opt-out capture and suppression.
- Recording disclosures where required.
- Human escalation for sensitive or complex cases.

## 6) Milestones (90-Day Plan)

> **Goal:** complete each milestone with measurable output before moving to the next phase.

### Milestone 1 — Foundation (Days 1–30)
**Deliverables**
- ICP definitions approved for 3 segments.
- 1 offer + 1 landing page per segment.
- Calling bot v1 scripts and QA rubric.
- CRM pipeline stages and dashboard setup.

**Exit criteria (must be complete)**
- Minimum 300 leads segmented and verified.
- 3 script variants tested internally.
- Funnel tracking active for all pipeline stages.

### Milestone 2 — Pilot (Days 31–60)
**Deliverables**
- 2 active campaigns (retailer-focused and factory-focused).
- Weekly experiment log with script and channel tests.
- 1 case study draft from pilot outcomes.

**Exit criteria (must be complete)**
- At least 40 qualified conversations.
- At least 15 booked meetings.
- Documented top 10 objections + final responses.

### Milestone 3 — Scale (Days 61–90)
**Deliverables**
- Expansion into 1–2 additional verticals or regions.
- Partner/referral motion live.
- Finalized sales playbook and onboarding SOP.

**Exit criteria (must be complete)**
- Repeatable weekly pipeline generation process.
- Positive ROI trend by qualified opportunity cost.
- Team-ready handoff docs for operations and sales.
